Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions that may help before starting the project.

  1. How long does it take to build a website?
    It depends on the project size, but a simple website usually takes between 5 and 10 business days.

  2. Do you design everything from scratch or use templates?
    I design everything from scratch, based on your style, identity, and business needs.

  3. Do I need to have photos and text ready?
    Not necessarily. If you don’t have them yet, I can use temporary images and help you organize the content.

  4. Is the website mobile‑friendly?
    Yes. All websites are fully responsive and adapted for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

  5. Are domain and hosting included?
    No. But I can guide you to choose the best option and configure everything for you.

  6. Can I request changes after the site is ready?
    Yes. You can choose one of the maintenance plans to keep receiving updates.

  7. Do you work with online stores?
    Yes. I build complete stores with products, categories, cart, and checkout.

  8. How does payment work?
    Usually 50% upfront and 50% upon delivery, but I can adapt depending on the project.

  9. Do you offer support after delivery?
    Yes. All maintenance plans include ongoing support.

  10. How do I get started?
    You can contact me via Instagram or email — I reply quickly and we can align everything.
